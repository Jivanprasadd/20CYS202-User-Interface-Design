Welcome to the Bank Kiosk

Please select from the following options:

  • Make a Deposit
  • Make a Withdrawal
  • Make a Transfer
  • Check Balance
  • Apply for a Loan
  • View Bank Offers

Make a Deposit

To make a deposit, please insert your debit card into the card reader and follow the prompts on the screen.

Make a Withdrawal

To make a withdrawal, please select the amount you would like to withdraw using the buttons on the screen, and then insert your debit card into the card reader and follow the prompts on the screen.

Make a Transfer

To make a transfer, please select the account you would like to transfer funds to using the buttons on the screen, enter the amount you would like to transfer, and then insert your debit card into the card reader and follow the prompts on the screen.

Check Balance

To check your account balance, please insert your debit card into the card reader and follow the prompts on the screen.
